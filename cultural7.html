Conclusion

As a conclusion, TV show like The Voice encourages a lot of people to chase their music dreams. But The Voice and all this reality television singing competition make people too crazy about being famous and even ignore the difficulty to become succeed. So many of them give up everything to chase their dreams but as a result lose everything and even lead to bankruptcy. So one solution may help solve the surplus of the musicians is to organize the company market. Create a job fair for everyone who is interested in music-related job, at the same time make the invite applications for music-related job fair, for instance put the end to nepotism. The second solution to help solve the surplus of the musicians is to tell the public the difficulty to become famous and avoid the public become too crazy to be famous. The best results are help to better the life for talented musicians and help the people who is not so good at music make the best choice for them. Moreover help the whole society be more stable.
